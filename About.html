About ZOË4U Foundation

Mission:

To raise generations who are able to fulfill their God given purpose and be partakers of God’s divine glory

Vision:

Raising young people in the fear of God

ZOË4U FOUNDATION is a well-organized group with motto “Auxano in Epignosis” meaning “Increase in Knowledge”.

It holds Seminars, Conferences and Summit which help the mindset of the youth.

ZOË4U FOUNDATION is a Christian fellowship that is open to all the youth in the world irrespective of their background. A team of trained experts in diversified disciplines of career run the foundation with its Cabinet members.

We aim at helping the youth to discover their God-given talents and purpose. This is achieved by the singular effort of the Holy Ghost who works in us.

Our Seminars, quarterly conferences and annual summits are geared towards raising the youth in the Word of God, character development and interpersonal skills that make them useful in the kingdom of God.
